The 4 Packages

Know these cold. Every price ties to an outcome.

Quick facts — come up on every call

  • Delivery speed: 7 to 14 days to a fully published, live website. A big chunk of that is just waiting on him to send photos, logo, and content — the faster he gets those to us, the faster he's live.
  • Contract: No contract, ever. Month-to-month, cancel anytime.
  • Client count: Early stage — Polished by Glizzy & Glam is the first real client. Own that honestly (see the "who have you done this for" objection card) — don't inflate it.

Each tier answers a different question: does he look professional, does he get found, does every lead get chased, or does he want to own it outright. Diagnose which one he's missing before you pitch.
